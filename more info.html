About us.

It started with an idea. Something Simple. To environmentally change NFTs for the better. One single transaction on the Ethereum Blockchain consumes 238.22 KiloWatts of energy. For reference, 100,000 VISA transactions require 148.63 KiloWatts of energy, which means that one transaction on the Ethereum Blockchain is equivalent to 160,277 VISA transactions. This insane amount of energy consumption creates an incredibly large carbon footprint, which in turn will only add to the number of greenhouse gases released into the atmosphere. Though the idea and concept of NFTs are great, the environmental damage is too large of a downfall. However, NovusPlanets promises to donate 15% of all funds towards charities that help the environment, whether that be through the prevention of deforestation, or by saving endangered animals. We aim and hope to make this world a better and safer place.

Our Hopes.

As a community, we strive for web3. We hope to steer away from the centralization of web2.0, and the lack of privacy involved in it. Web3 is a decentralized idea for the World Wide Web, where there are no 'leaders' or anyone to 'steal' your data and sell it to third parties or other groups. We aim for this future where everyone is safe and happy browsing the internet.
